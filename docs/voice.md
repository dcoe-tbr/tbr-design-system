# Design voice

Short version: **TBR is a publication, not a product.** When in doubt, design like you're laying out a newspaper — one that respects the reader and doesn't sell to them.

## Who it's for

The Brand Report covers outdoor sport and lifestyle brands (running, cycling, trail, climbing, ski, skate) with a business lens. Readers are operators: founders, marketing leads, retail buyers, investors. They read for information, not inspiration. They want signal over spectacle.

## What the design should feel like

| Feels right | Feels wrong |
|---|---|
| Financial Times + Monocle | Instagram, TikTok lifestyle |
| Bloomberg + The Atlantic | Generic SaaS dashboard |
| Considered, asymmetric | Centered, symmetric, uniform |
| Quiet, confident | Loud, decorative |
| Newsreader set large | Montserrat, Poppins, rounded sans |
| One orange accent | Rainbow category colors |
| Parchment paper background | Pure white everywhere |
| Left-aligned body | Centered body paragraphs |
| Documentary photos | Stock illustration, emoji |

## The core constraints

1. **One accent per view.** Orange is a highlight, not a wallpaper.
2. **Two typefaces.** Newsreader + Inter. Never more.
3. **Two page backgrounds.** Parchment for editorial, white for product.
4. **Small radii, soft shadows.** TBR isn't a lifestyle app.
5. **Left-aligned by default.** Centered only for marketing hero headlines or card grids with short copy.

## Writing cues that match the design

The words and the design reinforce each other. If the design feels corporate, the copy probably went corporate too.

- Headlines are declarative. "On's growth stalled. Here's why." not "On Running's Journey of Growth and Innovation"
- Eyebrows are short and categorical. SECTOR. EARNINGS. RETAIL.
- Bylines are simple. "Dan Coe · April 19, 2026"
- No corporate filler ("In today's fast-paced world…"). No hype ("We're thrilled to announce…").

## When in doubt

Picture the page printed on newsprint. If it still reads like a publication — not an ad or a dashboard — you're on track.
